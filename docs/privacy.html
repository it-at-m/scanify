2. Description of the Application

The Scanify flutter application is a powerful camera based scanner for all common QR and barcode formats. Scanify is originally developed by a student as a project at Hof University (Hochschule Hof).

3. Camera Permission and Device Data

Camera permission is strictly required for the core functionality of reading QR codes and barcodes. All camera data and scanned content are processed locally on your device in real time. The application does not record video, take unauthorized photos, or transmit the camera feed to any external servers.

4. Data Collection and Sharing

Scanify does not collect, store, process, or share any personal data. All scanned data displayed in the app is handled locally on the device and is never transmitted to the developer, any server, or any third party.

5. User Rights (GDPR)

Users possess rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including the right of access, rectification, and erasure. However, because Scanify does not collect or retain any personal data, requests to access, modify, or delete personal data cannot be fulfilled, as no such data exists on our systems.

General inquiries can be sent to the contact address listed above.
